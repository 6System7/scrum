About Scrum

Simply put we created Scrum to help reduce food wastage. Food wastage is a growing concern as many people are without food in not only in third world countries. But even on our own streets. Therefore we made it as easy as possible to get the food to those who need it. Deep down we all want to doo good and help but we don't always have the time or ability too so we made it easy for everyone. Register with us today and start helping us work towards a better future.
